about

Kerry is a front-end web developer and new media journalist in Toronto. She is currently a full-time front-end development student at HackerYou and also holds a bachelor of journalism degree from Ryerson University, where she majored in print journalism.

Her previous work experience includes six years at CBCNews.ca in a variety of roles, including web content production and web development, and Ryerson University.

Kerry lives in Toronto with her husband and their extensive book collection. In her spare time she enjoys knitting, attempting a running comeback and working her way through the RCM piano curriculum.
