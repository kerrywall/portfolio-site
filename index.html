about

Kerry is a front-end web developer and new media journalist in Toronto. She is currently a full-time front-end development student at HackerYou. She also holds a bachelor of journalism degree from Ryerson University, where she majored in print journalism.

Her previous work experience includes six years at CBCNews.ca in a variety of roles, including web content production and web development. Her projects included producing interactives and photo galleries and covering social media issues during the 2008 and 2011 federal election campaigns. She was also the digital project lead on CBCNews.ca's coverage of Parks Canada's 2012 search for the Franklin expedition wrecks, and held a high-impact producer contract between 2012 and 2013.

Kerry lives in Toronto with her husband and their extensive book collection. In her spare time she enjoys needlework, attempting to resume running after a two-year hiatus, and working her way through the RCM piano curriculum.
